"Dream is not that which you see while sleeping it is something that does not let your sleep."

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam

15th October 1931 - 27th July 2015

From born and raised in a small town of Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu to a reknown Scientist and the former Preisdent Abdul Kalam is an inspiration for millions of people not only in India but also in the world. Widely referred as the "People's President" he also a good educationist, writer and a public servant. For his involment as a scientist and science administration in DRDO, ISRO, Indian's civilian space programme and military developmet efforts he is called the Missile Man of India.
