"Dream is not that which you see while sleeping, it is something that does not let you sleep."

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam

15th October 1931 - 27th July 2015

From born and raised in a small town of Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu to a renown Scientist and the former President Abdul Kalam is an inspiration for millions of people not only in India but also around the world. Widely referred as the "People's President" he also a good educationist, writer and a public servant. For his involvement as a scientist and science administration in DRDO, ISRO, Indian's civilian space programme and military development efforts he is also called the Missile Man of India.
